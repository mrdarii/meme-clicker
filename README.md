📄 README — Read Me

Meme Clicker is a simple and fun clicker game where you tap a button and earn memes 😂

🎮 How to play:
Click the big MEME button
Earn memes for every click
Buy upgrades to increase your power
Get stronger and earn memes faster
⬆️ Upgrades:
Upgrade 1 → +1 meme per click (cost increases over time)
Upgrade 2 → +2 memes per click (cost increases by +40 each time)
📱 Controls:
Works on PC (mouse click)
Works on mobile (tap with finger)
Works on tablets
🔊 Features:
Click sound effects
Simple and clean UI
Fast progression
Lightweight and smooth gameplay
